Overview

This is a project that I worked on for three years, starting in my sophomore year of high school. In this project, I worked with Dr. Jason Keleher, Professor James Hofmann, and graduate students from Lewis University. This project was studying to find a way to prevent laser strikes on airplanes by using liquid crystal technologies.
